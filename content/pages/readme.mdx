## What this page is

This **page is a placeholder**for the kind of context that does not fit neatly inside a case study, note, or project card. Over time, I want it to feel like a lightweight operating manual: part introduction, part philosophy, part orientation for anyone landing here.

For now, I want it to be a simple place to explain how I approach design.

what I pay attention to, and what kind of work I naturally gravitate toward.

## How I like to work

I usually work best when the problem is clear, the writing is honest, and the decisions are documented well enough that someone else can step in without friction. I like structure, but not ceremony for its own sake. I care about clarity, momentum, and making sure the work still feels thoughtful once the novelty wears off.

I also tend to work in layers. I like zooming out to understand the shape of the problem, then getting very specific about the details that actually affect the experience.

## What this site is for

This site will slowly become a mix of project documentation, process notes, in-progress thoughts, and small reflections that would otherwise stay scattered across drafts, screenshots, and unfinished folders. Some parts will be polished, some will stay a little raw, and that balance is intentional.

I want this place to feel useful first and performative second.
