## Current focus

Right now this page is standing in as a living snapshot rather than a finished manifesto. I want it to be the place where current priorities stay visible: what I am building, what I am refining, and what I keep returning to when I have a little uninterrupted time.

At the moment, that mostly means improving this portfolio, tightening the editing workflow, and making the site feel more like a usable personal publishing system than a static showcase.

## What I am learning

I am spending time thinking about interface clarity, editorial systems, and how small interaction details change the feel of a product. I am also paying closer attention to tools and workflows that let me publish faster without making the output feel rushed.

The goal is not just to make things look better, but to make the process of updating and maintaining them feel lighter.

## What is in the background

Alongside active work, I usually have a few slower threads running in the background: things I am reading, ideas I have not formed fully yet, and small experiments that may or may not turn into something public later.

This page will eventually become the place where those threads feel visible without needing a full note every time.
